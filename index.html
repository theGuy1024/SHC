President’s Remarks

Greetings to all stakeholders of Sacred Heart College!

Sacred Heart College, a Catholic Institution owned by the Catholic Diocese is pleased to have you an integral part of our family. We certainly continue to lift the bar in all aspects of our student’s education. SHC lives its mission in developing our students holistically, by fostering good values, morals, and a sound Catholic Education. SHC, surely provides a Christ centered environment, where CHRIST matters, and as His unique creation we give thanks to Him for the masterpiece He created, in His image and likeness. Our well educated faculty, and well trained staff, surely provide a nurturing, caring, safe and conducive environment for learning to all our students. Our mission is to collectively build the prestige of SHC, with the collaboration of all stakeholders in education. SHC, is the premier institution of first choice in the Western Region and the nation as a whole. We offer business programs, science programs, General Studies programs to cater to the needs and choices of our students. We continue to excel in the areas of academics, sports, contests, spirituality and many others. We invite you to join our SHC family and visit our web site to know more about us and what we offer.
